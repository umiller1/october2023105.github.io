Daily Fun Fact

— All clownfish are born male and can potentially change to female.

Usually, clownfish live in groups with a dominant male and female, and then 0-4 juvenile males. When the dominant female dies, the dominant male changes sex to become the dominant female and one of the juveniles become the dominant male.
