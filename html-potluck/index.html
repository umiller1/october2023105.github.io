Bais Semantic Ramen



Ingredients

  • Header Element — 1lbs
  • Nav Element — 1lbs
  • Aside Element — 1lbs
  • Main Element — 1lbs
  • Section Element — 2lbs
  • Article Element — 2lbs
  • Footer Element — 1lbs

Directions

  1. Draw a sketch of how you want your ramensite to look layout wise.
  2. Create a header element and apply CSSeasings to your taste.
  3. Depending on how you prepared your header, wrap the header around a nav element or prepare your nav element beneath your header. Add complimentary csseasings.
  4. Underneath your header and/or nav, add a main element.
  5. Make an empty space in the center of your main so you can add other elements.
  6. Add an aside element first. Add 1bs of a section beneath it. Add 2bs of an article beneath the section.
  7. In the second lbs of article, add 1lbs of section.
  8. Under main
